GratefulTime.app

Key features include:

  • Daily notifications based on preferred time and time zone
  • Entry of three gratitude items plus a customizable prompt
  • Calendar view to browse and review past entries
  • Adjustable settings for unlock time and time zone
  • Secure Sign In with Apple authentication with minimal data storage
  • AI-generated monthly summaries of your gratitude entries
  • Progress tracking with a monthly completion progress bar
  • Sleek, modern UI with dark mode and minimalistic design

Available exclusively on iOS.

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

GratefulTime.app ("the App") prioritizes your privacy and data security. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User Provided Information: Upon signing in with Apple, we obtain minimal authentication data. If you consent, we store your email address, full name, and time zone.

2.2 Automatic Data: No automatic collection of personal data beyond device time zone required for notifications.

3. Use of Information

Information is used solely to authenticate, personalize, schedule notifications, generate AI summaries, and improve the app securely.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store minimal data securely, without sharing with third parties, adhering to Apple's privacy standards.

5. User Control and Rights

  • You control the information you provide.
  • You can update or delete your personal info in settings or by contacting support.
  • Opting out of optional data does not affect app use.
  • Deleting the app or account removes stored data.

6. Notifications

Local notifications are sent at your preferred time and can be disabled in device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy; users will be notified of significant changes.
